Criteria

Why me?

When building high-quality web interfaces, I distinguish four criteria:

  • Reliability

    Change your web interface as you wish - it will withstand everything! Text, its amount; images; number of menu items; blocks, they are bigger, smaller, here they are, and then they are not... It all depends on your desire!

  • Convenience

    Your web interface will adapt to any device and will always be convenient to use! This is a very important criterion, because you order a website in order to increase sales of your product, and without ease of use, this is impossible!

  • Optimisation

    Despite the fact that the web has reached such a level of development that the speed of web interfaces is losing its sharpness, it is still important. It's simple: the faster your website works, the more money you will earn!

  • Accessibility

    Helps search engines and people with disabilities to understand information from your web interface. If your site is written correctly, even visually impaired people will be able to hear what your web interface is about, its main points and even a description of the images in it.
